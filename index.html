Hi I'm Simon.

Imaginative. Problem Solver. Abstract Thinker. Open-Minded. Objective. Unusual challenges excite me. Passionate about design, development, innovative tech and the internet. Looking to work in the information technology and business industries.

I am a motivated student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Management and Technology at Syracuse University's School of Information Studies. I am an active member of the university community and serve as the Vice President for BeIT, the iSchools ungraduate organization. I am also involved in Active Minds, the Syracude University Outdoors Club, Phi Delta Theta Fraternity, and WERW Radio. I serve as a peer advisor to underclassmen in the iSchool. I have worked as an Assistant Technician for 4 years in the Network Wiring services division of ITS at Syracuse University.

I hope to pursue a career that merges the industries of information technology and business in New York City. I want to help companies create effective, intuitive, and aesthetically pleasing interfaces that are accessible users from all walks of life. I am passionate about the intersection between graphic design and web development.
