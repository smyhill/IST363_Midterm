Hi I'm Simon.

Imaginative. Problem Solver. Abstract Thinker. Open-Minded. Objective. Unusual challenges excite me. I am passionate about design, development, innovative technology and the internet.


I am a motivated student pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in Information Management and Technology at Syracuse University's School of Information Studies. I am an active member of the university community and serve as the Vice President for BeIT, the iSchools professional organization and act as an iSchool Peer Advisor. I am also involved in Active Minds, the Syracuse University Outdoors Club, Phi Delta Theta Fraternity, and WERW Radio. I have worked as an Assistant Technician for four years in the Network Wiring Services division of ITS at Syracuse University.


I hope to pursue a career that merges the industries of information technology and business in New York City. I want to help companies create effective, intuitive, and aesthetically pleasing interfaces that are accessible to users from all walks of life. I hope to further the intersection between graphic design and web development.
